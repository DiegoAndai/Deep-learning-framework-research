## Protocol for model parameters evaluation:

### Metrics:
- **Accuracy**
- **Precision mean**
- **Recall mean**
- **Relatedness**

### Parameters:
- **Embedding dimensionality** (600 | 900 | 1200 | 1500 | 1800)
- **Learning rate** (1 | 0.1 | 0.01 | 0.001)
- **Vocabulary size** (30.000 | 50.000 | 70.000)
- **Window size** (1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5)
